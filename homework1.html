Amazing Brownie Recipe


This is a guide to make an amazing gooey brownie. Make sure to follow this step by step guide on how to make this delicious brownie.


Shopping List

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt

Cooking Instructions

This will take approximately 25 mins to cook. Additionally will need extra 20 mins to prep.

  1. Step 1:
    • Pre heat the oven to 175 on fan
    • Heat up some butter until melted
  2. Step 2:
    • Into a bowl add:
      • 1 cup sugar
      • 2 Whole eggs
      • 1 Teaspoon of vanilla
  3. Step 3:
    • Into another bowl add:
      • half cup of flour
      • 1/3 cup of Cocoa
      • Teaspoon of baking powder
      • Half teaspoon of salt
  4. Step 4:
    • Now combine both bowls made in step 2 & 3
    • Along with the melted butter and mix!
  5. Step 5:
    • Now place the mixture into a baking tray
    • Cook for 25mins until soft and gooey inside
